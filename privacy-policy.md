# Privacy Policy for Scanner Pro

**Last Updated:** April 14, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application **Scanner Pro** ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application is a QR and Barcode scanner that provides a leveled user experience.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
### Personal Data
The Application **does not** collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or phone number) to external servers. All scanned data and history are stored locally on your device's internal database.

### Non-Personal Data
We may use non-personal information to improve the Application. This includes:
* **Device Information:** Model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for optimization.
* **Usage Statistics:** How often the app is opened and which features are used.

## 2. Permissions
To function correctly, the Application requires the following permissions:
* **Camera:** To scan QR and Barcodes.
* **Storage:** To save and share QR codes as images.
* **Internet:** To show advertisements and verify connectivity.

## 3. Advertising (Google AdMob)
This Application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs) to show personalized ads. 
* You can manage your ad preferences via your device settings.
* For more information, please visit [Google’s Advertising Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 4. User Progress (Levels and Scores)
Your level (A1 to C2) and scan scores are stored locally in the Application’s database. This data is not shared with third parties.

## 5. Security
We value your trust in providing us your information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
