Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Scanner Pro handles your information. By using the Application, you agree to these terms.

1. Local Data Privacy

All scanned data, history logs, and user progress (Level A1-C2 and Scores) are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to any external servers.

2. Permissions

  • Camera: Required to scan QR/Barcodes.
  • Storage: Required to save and share images.
  • Internet: Required for ads and connection status.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads. They may collect device identifiers and IP addresses to show relevant ads. You can opt-out via your device settings.

4. Global Compliance

We comply with GDPR and CCPA standards as we do not collect personal information on our own servers.

5. Contact

For inquiries, please contact us through our official GitHub repository.
