Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Scanner Pro Master collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

1. Data Collection and Usage

To provide a high-quality experience, we use several integrated services:

  • Local Storage: Your scan history and custom QR data are stored only on your device's internal database. We do not have access to your personal scans.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for backend analytics, crash reporting, and cloud messaging. This helps us monitor app performance and fix bugs in real-time.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal data such as device model, OS version, and language settings to optimize app performance.

2. Use of Firebase Services

Our application utilizes Firebase for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve feature accessibility.
  • Crashlytics: To identify technical issues and ensure application stability.
  • Remote Config: To update app behavior and appearance without requiring a new download.

3. Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (IDFA/AAID) to provide personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out through your device's system settings.

4. Permissions Required

  • Camera: To scan and decode QR/Barcodes.
  • Storage: To save generated QR codes as images and access gallery for scanning.
  • Internet: To sync with Firebase and load advertisements.

5. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Under global data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the data stored on your device.
  • Delete all local data by clearing app cache or uninstalling the app.
  • Opt-out of personalized tracking via OS-level privacy settings.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, since most data is stored locally, the security of your device is your primary responsibility.
